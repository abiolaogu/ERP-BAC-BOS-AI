## Account Creation

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the NEXUS Platform**
   - Navigate to your organization's NEXUS URL (e.g., `https://nexus.yourcompany.com`)
   - Or use the public instance at `https://nexus.app`

2. **Click "Sign Up"**
   - Located in the top-right corner of the login page

3. **Enter Your Information**
   - **Full Name**: Your first and last name
   - **Email Address**: Use your work or personal email
   - **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, and numbers)
   - **Organization** (optional): Enter your company name or leave blank

4. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email inbox for a verification message
   - Click the verification link (link expires in 24 hours)
   - You'll be redirected back to NEXUS

5. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Upload a profile picture (optional)
   - Set your time zone
   - Configure notification preferences

### Password Requirements

Your password must meet these requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters long
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (@, #, $, !, etc.)

**Example**: `MySecure#Pass2025`

## Logging In

### Standard Login

1. **Navigate to NEXUS**
   - Go to your organization's NEXUS URL

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - **Email**: Your registered email address
   - **Password**: Your account password
   - Click **"Sign In"**

3. **Two-Factor Authentication** (if enabled)
   - Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
   - Or enter the code sent to your email/SMS

### Single Sign-On (SSO)

If your organization uses SSO:

1. Click **"Sign in with SSO"**
2. Enter your organization's domain
3. You'll be redirected to your identity provider (Google, Microsoft, etc.)
4. Authenticate with your existing corporate credentials
5. You'll be redirected back to NEXUS

### Remember Me

- Check "Remember Me" to stay logged in for 30 days
- Only use this on personal, secure devices
- **Do not** use on shared or public computers

### Forgot Password

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### Main Components

#### 1. Top Navigation Bar
- **NEXUS Logo**: Click to return to the Hub
- **Search Bar**: Search across all your documents, files, and emails
- **Notifications**: View recent activity and alerts
- **Profile Menu**: Access settings, help, and logout

#### 2. Recent Documents
- View recently accessed or edited documents

#### 3. Quick Actions
- **New Document**: Create a Writer document
- **New Spreadsheet**: Create a Sheets spreadsheet
- **New Presentation**: Create a Slides presentation
- **Upload Files**: Add files to Drive
- **Start Meeting**: Launch a video conference

#### 4. Application Launcher
- Click any app icon to launch that application
- Hover to see app description
- Right-click for quick actions (New document, Open recent, etc.)

#### 5. Activity Feed
- See recent changes to shared documents
- View comments and mentions
- Track meeting notifications
- See file sharing activity

#### 6. Calendar Widget
- View upcoming events and meetings
- Click to open full calendar
- See today's schedule at a glance

### Using the Search Bar

The global search lets you find anything in NEXUS:

1. **Click the search bar** or press `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+K` (Mac)
2. **Type your query**
   - Document names: "Q4 Report"
   - Content: "budget forecast"
   - People: "@john.doe"
   - Dates: "last week"
3. **Filter results**
   - By app: "in:writer budget"
   - By person: "from:jane.smith"
   - By date: "before:2025-01-01"
4. **Click a result** to open it

### Notifications

Click the bell icon to see:
- **Document updates**: Someone edited a shared file
- **Comments**: New comments or @mentions
- **Meetings**: Upcoming or starting meetings
- **Shares**: Files or folders shared with you
- **System alerts**: Important platform updates

## Accessing Applications

### Launching an Application

There are several ways to open NEXUS applications:

#### Method 1: Application Launcher (Hub)
1. From the Hub dashboard
2. Click the application icon (Writer, Sheets, Slides, etc.)
3. The app opens in a new tab or window

#### Method 2: Quick Actions Menu
1. Click the **"+"** button in the top navigation
2. Select the type of document to create
3. The app opens with a new blank document

#### Method 3: Direct URL
Each app has its own URL:
- Writer: `https://nexus.yourcompany.com/writer`
- Sheets: `https://nexus.yourcompany.com/sheets`
- Slides: `https://nexus.yourcompany.com/slides`
- Drive: `https://nexus.yourcompany.com/drive`
- Meet: `https://nexus.yourcompany.com/meet`

#### Method 4: Keyboard Shortcuts
- `Ctrl+Shift+D` - Open Writer
- `Ctrl+Shift+S` - Open Sheets
- `Ctrl+Shift+P` - Open Slides
- `Ctrl+Shift+F` - Open Drive
- `Ctrl+Shift+M` - Open Meet

### Opening Recent Documents

1. **From the Hub**
   - Recent documents appear on the main dashboard
   - Click any document to open it

2. **From within an app**
   - File â†’ Open Recent
   - Shows last 10 documents you opened

3. **From Search**
   - Type the document name in the search bar
   - Click the result to open

### Accessing Settings

1. Click your **profile picture** in the top-right corner

3. Or use the keyboard shortcut: `Ctrl+,` (Comma)

### Settings Categories

- Phone number
- Profile picture
- Job title
- Department

**To update your profile:**
1. Go to Settings â†’ Profile
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Save"

#### 2. Account Security

**Password**
- Click "Change Password"
- Enter current password
- Enter new password twice
- Click "Update Password"

## Troubleshooting

### Can't log in?
- Verify your email and password are correct
- Check Caps Lock is off
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Contact your administrator

### Not receiving emails?
- Check spam/junk folder
- Add noreply@nexusplatform.io to contacts
- Verify email address in settings
- Check notification preferences

### App not loading?
- Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check internet connection
- Contact support if problem persists
