# Video Script: NEXUS Platform Overview

**Duration**: 5 minutes
**Target Audience**: New users, decision makers

---

## Opening (0:00-0:30)

**[SCREEN: NEXUS logo animation]**

**NARRATOR**: "Welcome to NEXUS - your complete productivity platform. In this 5-minute overview, we'll show you how NEXUS brings together everything your team needs to collaborate and get work done."

**[SCREEN: Montage of people working on different devices]**

---

## Section 1: What is NEXUS? (0:30-1:30)

**[SCREEN: NEXUS Hub dashboard]**

**NARRATOR**: "NEXUS is a comprehensive, self-hosted platform that combines all your productivity tools in one place. Think of it as your own private Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace."

**[SCREEN: Icons of applications appearing]**

"With NEXUS, you get:
- **Writer** - Create and edit documents
- **Sheets** - Build powerful spreadsheets
- **Slides** - Design presentations
- **Drive** - Store and share files
- **Meet** - Host video conferences
- And much more..."

**[SCREEN: Split screen showing multiple apps]**

"Best of all? Your data stays on your servers. Complete control, complete privacy."

---

## Section 2: Creating Content (1:30-2:30)

**[SCREEN: Creating a new document]**

**NARRATOR**: "Let's create a document. Just click 'New Document' and start writing."

**[SCREEN: Typing in document, showing formatting]**

"Format text with a click. Add images, tables, and charts. Everything auto-saves, so you never lose your work."

**[SCREEN: Quick demonstration of Sheets]**

"Need a spreadsheet? NEXUS Sheets gives you powerful formulas, beautiful charts, and pivot tables."

**[SCREEN: Quick demonstration of Slides]**

"Creating a presentation? Slides offers professional themes and easy drag-and-drop editing."

---

## Section 3: Real-Time Collaboration (2:30-3:30)

**[SCREEN: Two users editing same document]**

**NARRATOR**: "But here's where NEXUS really shines - real-time collaboration."

**[SCREEN: Colored cursors showing different users]**

"See your teammates' cursors as they edit. Changes appear instantly. Add comments and suggestions. It's like being in the same room."

**[SCREEN: Starting a video meeting]**

"Need to discuss? Start a video meeting right from your document with one click."

**[SCREEN: Video call with screen sharing]**

"Share your screen, present your slides, and collaborate face-to-face - all in HD quality with up to 100 participants."

---

## Section 4: File Management (3:30-4:15)

**[SCREEN: NEXUS Drive interface]**

**NARRATOR**: "Store everything in NEXUS Drive. Organize files in folders, share with your team, and access from anywhere."

**[SCREEN: Sharing a file]**

"Sharing is simple. Just click 'Share,' choose permissions, and send. You can share with individuals or get a link to share with anyone."

**[SCREEN: Mobile and desktop apps]**

"Access your files from web, mobile, or desktop. Changes sync instantly across all devices."

---

## Section 5: Security & Control (4:15-4:45)

**[SCREEN: Security features graphic]**

**NARRATOR**: "Security is built-in. Enterprise-grade encryption protects your data at rest and in transit."

**[SCREEN: Admin panel]**

"Administrators get complete control. Manage users, set permissions, configure SSO, and monitor usage - all from one dashboard."

**[SCREEN: Self-hosted server graphic]**

"Because NEXUS is self-hosted, your data never leaves your infrastructure. You own it, you control it."

---

## Closing (4:45-5:00)

**[SCREEN: NEXUS Hub with all apps]**

**NARRATOR**: "NEXUS - everything your team needs in one powerful, secure platform."

**[SCREEN: Website URL and CTA]**

"Ready to get started? Visit nexusplatform.io to try NEXUS today."

**[SCREEN: NEXUS logo]**

**NARRATOR**: "NEXUS. Work better, together."

---

## Visual Notes

- **Style**: Modern, clean, professional
- **Music**: Upbeat, optimistic background music
- **Graphics**: Smooth transitions, minimal text overlays
- **Pace**: Fast enough to maintain interest, slow enough to be clear
- **Screen recordings**: Real UI, not mockups
- **Call to action**: Clear and prominent at end

## Key Messages

1. NEXUS is comprehensive (all tools in one)
2. Real-time collaboration is seamless
3. Self-hosted means control and privacy
4. Easy to use for everyone
5. Enterprise-ready security
