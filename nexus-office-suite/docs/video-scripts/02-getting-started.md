# Video Script: Getting Started with NEXUS

**Duration**: 10 minutes
**Target Audience**: New users

---

## Opening (0:00-0:30)

**NARRATOR**: "Welcome to NEXUS! In this tutorial, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to get started - from creating your account to your first video meeting."

---

## Account Setup (0:30-2:00)

**[SCREEN: Sign-up page]**

**NARRATOR**: "First, let's create your account. Enter your email, create a strong password, and click Sign Up."

**[SHOW: Filling out signup form]**

"Check your email for a verification link and click it."

**[SHOW: Email verification screen]**

"Great! Now let's set up your profile. Add a profile picture and set your timezone."

**[SHOW: Profile settings]**

---

## Navigating the Hub (2:00-4:00)

**[SCREEN: Hub dashboard]**

**NARRATOR**: "This is the Hub - your central dashboard. Let's take a tour."

**[HIGHLIGHT each section as mentioned]**

"At the top, you'll find:
- **Search** - Find anything instantly (press Ctrl+K)
- **Notifications** - See recent activity
- **Your profile** - Access settings

Below, you'll see:
- **Recent documents** - Quick access to files you've worked on
- **Quick actions** - Create new documents, spreadsheets, or presentations
- **Applications** - Launch any NEXUS app
- **Activity feed** - See what's happening with your team"

---

## Creating Your First Document (4:00-6:00)

**[SCREEN: Creating document]**

**NARRATOR**: "Let's create your first document. Click 'New Document.'"

**[SHOW: Document editor]**

"Here's the editor. Type to start writing. Use the toolbar to format text - bold, italic, headings, and more."

**[SHOW: Formatting text]**

"Add images by clicking Insert â†’ Image. Create tables for structured data."

**[SHOW: Adding image and table]**

"Notice the document auto-saves - see the 'Saved' indicator? You never have to worry about losing your work."

---

## Sharing and Collaboration (6:00-8:00)

**[SCREEN: Share dialog]**

**NARRATOR**: "Let's share this document. Click the Share button."

**[SHOW: Entering email and setting permissions]**

"Enter a colleague's email, choose their permission level - Viewer, Commenter, or Editor - and send."

**[SHOW: Two cursors in document]**

"When they open the document, you'll see their cursor. Type together in real-time!"

**[SHOW: Adding a comment]**

"Add comments by selecting text and clicking the comment button. Great for feedback and discussions."

---

## Starting a Meeting (8:00-9:30)

**[SCREEN: Starting a meeting]**

**NARRATOR**: "Need to chat face-to-face? Click 'Start Meeting.'"

**[SHOW: Meeting preview screen]**

"Check your camera and microphone, then click 'Join now.'"

**[SHOW: In meeting]**

"You're in! Control your mic and camera with these buttons. Share your screen to present. Use chat to send messages."

**[SHOW: Screen sharing]**

"Sharing your screen is perfect for presenting that document you just created."

---

## Closing (9:30-10:00)

**[SCREEN: Hub dashboard]**

**NARRATOR**: "That's it! You now know the basics:
- Create documents, spreadsheets, and presentations
- Collaborate in real-time
- Share with your team
- Meet face-to-face"

**[SCREEN: CTA]**

"Explore more in our user guides at docs.nexusplatform.io. Happy collaborating!"
