## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the NEXUS Business Operating System. This manual guides end users through the day-to-day features of the platform, including CRM, finance, project management, office productivity, AI copilots, and communication tools.

### 1.1 Who Is This Manual For?
- Business owners managing their operations through NEXUS
- Sales representatives using CRM features
- Finance team members processing payments and invoices
- HR personnel managing employee records
- Project managers tracking tasks and milestones
- Any team member using the office suite for daily work

---

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Logging In
1. Open your browser and navigate to your organization's NEXUS URL (e.g., `https://yourcompany.nexus.bac.cloud`)
2. Enter your email address and password
3. If MFA is enabled, complete the second factor verification
4. You are directed to your personalized dashboard

### 2.2 Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard displays:
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to frequently used features
- **Recent Activity**: Your latest actions across all modules
- **Notifications**: Unread messages, task assignments, reminders
- **AI Assistant**: Quick access to the AI copilot for instant help
- **Calendar Widget**: Today's events and upcoming meetings

### 2.3 Navigation
The left sidebar provides access to all enabled modules:
- **Home**: Dashboard and notifications
- **CRM**: Contacts, leads, opportunities, pipeline
- **Finance**: Invoices, payments, expenses
- **Projects**: Tasks, Kanban boards, milestones
- **HR**: Employee directory, leave requests, attendance
- **Inventory**: Products, stock levels, warehouses
- **Marketing**: Campaigns, templates, analytics
- **Support**: Tickets, knowledge base
- **Office**: Mail, Drive, Writer, Sheets, Slides, Calendar, Chat, Meet

## 3. CRM Module

### 3.1 Managing Contacts
1. Navigate to **CRM > Contacts**
2. Click **+ New Contact** to add a contact
3. Fill in details: name, email, phone, company, custom fields, tags
4. Click **Save** to create the contact
5. Use the search bar and filters to find existing contacts
6. Click any contact to view details, activity history, and associated deals

### 3.2 Working with Leads
1. Navigate to **CRM > Leads**
2. Create a new lead or import from CSV
3. Qualify leads by updating their status (New, Contacted, Qualified, Unqualified)
4. Convert qualified leads to opportunities using the **Convert** button
5. Track conversion metrics in the Leads Dashboard

### 3.3 Sales Pipeline
1. Navigate to **CRM > Pipeline**
2. View opportunities on the visual pipeline board
3. Drag opportunities between stages (Prospecting, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed Won/Lost)
4. Click an opportunity to update value, expected close date, and notes
5. Use pipeline filters to focus on specific time periods or sales reps

---

## 4. Finance Module

### 4.1 Creating Invoices
1. Navigate to **Finance > Invoices**
2. Click **+ New Invoice**
3. Select or create a customer, add line items with descriptions, quantities, and rates
4. Apply tax rates and discounts as needed
5. Click **Send** to email the invoice directly to the customer
6. Track invoice status: Draft, Sent, Viewed, Paid, Overdue

### 4.2 Processing Payments
1. When a customer pays via the invoice payment link, the system automatically records the payment
2. For manual payments, navigate to **Finance > Payments > Record Payment**
3. Select the invoice, enter amount and payment method
4. The invoice status updates automatically

### 4.3 Viewing Financial Reports
1. Navigate to **Finance > Reports**
2. Available reports: Revenue summary, accounts receivable, payment history
3. Filter by date range, customer, or payment method
4. Export reports in PDF or CSV format

---

## 5. Projects Module

### 5.1 Creating Projects
1. Navigate to **Projects > All Projects**
2. Click **+ New Project**
3. Enter project name, description, start/end dates, and team members
4. Choose a view: Kanban, List, Gantt, or Calendar

### 5.2 Managing Tasks
1. Within a project, click **+ Add Task**
2. Set title, description, assignee, priority, due date, and tags
3. On the Kanban board, drag tasks between columns (To Do, In Progress, Review, Done)
4. Add comments and attachments to tasks for team collaboration
5. Track time spent on tasks using the built-in timer

### 5.3 Milestones and Sprints
1. Define milestones as major project checkpoints
2. Create sprints for time-boxed work periods
3. Assign tasks to sprints and track sprint velocity
4. Review sprint progress on the Sprint Dashboard

---

## 6. HR Module

### 6.1 Employee Directory
1. Navigate to **HR > Employees**
2. Browse the employee directory with search and department filters
3. View individual profiles: contact info, role, department, reporting structure

### 6.2 Leave Requests
1. Navigate to **HR > Leave**
2. Click **Request Leave** to submit a new request
3. Select leave type (annual, sick, personal), dates, and add notes
4. Submit for manager approval
5. Track request status: Pending, Approved, Rejected

### 6.3 Time and Attendance
1. Navigate to **HR > Attendance**
2. Clock in/out using the web interface or mobile app
3. View your attendance history and hours worked
4. Submit overtime requests when applicable
5. Managers can review and approve team attendance records

---

## 7. Office Suite

### 7.1 Mail
1. Click **Office > Mail** to access your inbox
2. Compose new emails with rich text formatting and attachments
3. Organize emails with folders, labels, and search
4. Set up email signatures and auto-replies

### 7.2 Drive
1. Click **Office > Drive** to access file storage
2. Upload files (drag and drop or click upload)
3. Create folders to organize your files
4. Share files or folders with team members (view, edit, or comment permissions)
5. Access version history for any file

### 7.3 Writer (Documents)
1. Click **Office > Writer** to create or open documents
2. Use the rich text editor with formatting toolbar
3. Collaborate in real-time: invite team members to co-edit
4. Export documents to PDF, DOCX, or HTML

### 7.4 Sheets (Spreadsheets)
1. Click **Office > Sheets** to create or open spreadsheets
2. Enter data in cells with support for formulas (SUM, AVERAGE, VLOOKUP, etc.)
3. Create charts and pivot tables for data visualization
4. Share and collaborate in real-time

### 7.5 Slides (Presentations)
1. Click **Office > Slides** to create or open presentations
2. Choose from available themes or start blank
3. Add slides with text, images, charts, and media
4. Present directly from the browser with presentation mode

### 7.6 Calendar
1. Click **Office > Calendar** to view your schedule
2. Create events with title, time, location, and attendees
3. Set reminders (notification, email)
4. View by day, week, or month
5. Sync with external calendars via CalDAV

### 7.7 Chat
1. Click **Office > Chat** for real-time messaging
2. Send direct messages or create group channels
3. Use threads for organized conversations
4. Share files and links within messages
5. Search message history

### 7.8 Meet (Video Conferencing)
1. Click **Office > Meet** to start or join a meeting
2. Share the meeting link with participants
3. Features: video, audio, screen sharing, recording
4. Schedule meetings through the Calendar module for automatic invitations

## 8. AI Copilot

### 8.1 Accessing the AI Assistant
1. Click the **AI** icon in the bottom-right corner of any page
2. Type your question or request in natural language

### 8.2 What the AI Copilot Can Do
- **CRM Queries**: "Show me all VIP contacts added this month"
- **Financial Insights**: "What is our revenue trend for the last quarter?"
- **HR Information**: "How many leave requests are pending approval?"
- **Project Updates**: "What tasks are overdue in the Marketing project?"
- **Document Drafting**: "Draft a follow-up email for the client meeting"
- **Data Analysis**: "Create a chart of sales by region for Q1"
- **General Help**: "How do I create a recurring invoice?"

### 8.3 AI Copilot Tips
- Be specific with your requests for better results
- The AI has access to your tenant's data (respecting your permission level)
- Use follow-up questions for deeper analysis
- The AI remembers context within a session for natural conversation flow

## 10. Marketing Module

### 10.1 Creating Campaigns
1. Navigate to **Marketing > Campaigns**
2. Click **+ New Campaign** and set the campaign name, type, and schedule
3. Select your target audience from CRM contacts
4. Choose a template or create custom content
5. Schedule or send immediately

### 10.2 Email Templates
1. Navigate to **Marketing > Templates**
2. Create reusable email templates with the visual editor
3. Use merge tags for personalization ({first_name}, {company}, etc.)

## 11. Support Module

### 11.1 Submitting Tickets
1. Navigate to **Support > My Tickets**
2. Click **+ New Ticket** to report an issue
3. Enter subject, description, priority, and attach screenshots if applicable
4. Track ticket status: Open, In Progress, Waiting, Resolved, Closed

### 11.2 Knowledge Base
1. Navigate to **Support > Knowledge Base**
2. Search for articles and guides
3. Browse by category for self-service help
